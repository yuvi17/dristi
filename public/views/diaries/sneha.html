SNEHA SINHA

Hello Everyone!
I am Sneha Sinha, batch of 2014, pursuing B.Pharm from B.I..T, Mesra. DRISTI for me, is not just a student club or an organisation, it has become an integral part of my life. It is much more than simply an opportunity to observe village life closely, we experience it, contribute to its betterment as well as gain from it. The sense of empowering anybody through sheer hard work and dedication has overwhelmed and humbled me at the same time, changing me for the better.
I joined DRISTI right after an induction programme by our seniors in which we were told about the amazing experiences all volunteers had had so far as a part of the DRISTI family. It was summed up in an expertly made video, which made me realise that the opportunities of working in DRISTI are not just restricted to social betterment, but also enhancing our own technical and public skills, networking with the college and even national student communities. All these realizations inspired me to work for such a cause that I came to understand was great it every sense.
I am a part of the education wing in DRISTI as I strongly believe in the sense of empowerment education brings with itself. I give weekly tuition classes every week to the children of Panchauli village. I have gained experience in designing curriculum modules and also experiment with including extra-curricular activities in classes so that my students which experience personality development as well.
I would like to thank DRISTI for making a sincere, responsible and vigilant citizen out of me. Thank you everything!
