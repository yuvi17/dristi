UZMA ALIA

I am Uzma Alia currently pursuing B.Pharm from B.I.T, MESRA.As a fresher, inquisitive as I ever was, I wanted to try my hands at several new things and the clubs offered experiences but DRISTI offered experience combined with contentment. My desire to be the jack of all trades encouraged me to join DRISTI. Those long walks from the college campus to Panchauli were self fulfilled when the kids actually remembered our names or became emotionally attached to us. Of course, it provided us immense satisfaction to be a factor that brought about change, but, above all, it groomed our skills, actually making us versatile. There was a lot to do- event management, resource gathering, trying to be entertaining from the point of view of children and the list goes on. I have always been provided the most wonderful of opportunities but I think of those underprivileged kids who by the use of such amenities might turn out to be even brighter and smarter than the rest of us. India needs a change and for that miraculous revolution to occur, the pillars of the nation ought to be strengthened with utmost priority. COLLABORATE! COALESCE! CONTINUE...
