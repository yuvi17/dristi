SAURAV JHA

This is Saurav Jha, a student of B.I.T, MESRA penning down my memorable experiences in DRISTI and how the people I work with are not just an amazing team but like a family to me now.
The reason for joining DRISTI was guided by my personal interpretation of ‘development’ which I believe should be as much for the self as much for those in a disadvantageous position in our society. This imparts an unparalleled sense of contentment and satisfaction, stemming from working for the underprivileged, unlike anything else I have experienced.
My first special DRISTI memory is of three little children whom were so excited to learn the basic concepts of addition and subtraction, my very first visit as a tutor to the nearby village. Their anxious eyes and voices were enough to foretell their thirst for knowledge. A lot has followed then after, a bouquet of feelings and emotions are attached with DRISTI for me and it has shaped a positive “revamp” in me. I have become more interactive and am not daunted by communication with new people I encounter in life.
The vision that DRISTI carries with itself is noble & pure and I am confident that it will be a means of upliftment of the country in its new role as an NGO. Hopefully, we will scale even greater heights in the future.
