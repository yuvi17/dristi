PRERNA

Hi all, this is Prerna, a proud DRISTIAN from B.I.T, MESRA. My very own “prerna” or inspiration behind joining DRISTI was the great opportunity to experience rural India and help it grow by incorporating both social and technical aspects of my education. I wanted to interact with the village folk, render my selfless service to them and be the reason for their happy smiles.
Before joining BIT, I had never imagined that I would get such amazing platform to convert my social work dream to reality. I have been a DRISTIan since Oct 2015 and I am on top of the world for its recent incorporation as a NGO.
I have benefitted immensely from this experience, gaining a lot experience in communication skills, management, creativity and teamwork. My motive was to make just few faces smile, but DRISTI changed my life forever and made me a better person. I am now extremely motivated to give my best in every endeavour and undoubtedly I will be serving with full enthusiasm in the days to come. In my opinion, working at individual level will one day surely lead our country to development. I strongly believe that helping others always makes you great but doing it selflessly makes you greater.
