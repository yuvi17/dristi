SOURAV SAMDARSHI

Hello! I am Saurav Samdarshi, a third year B.Tech student of B.I.T, MESRA.
It all started in my first year when I decided to join DRISTI. At first it was the induction by my enthusiastic seniors which moved me. I still remember that chilly winter evening when I was in the college building, all curious and excited about this different venture, (and honestly welcoming an excuse to venture out of textbooks into the real world!) to know what it was all about.
After joining DRISTI, I realised it isn’t so easy to go into a village and teach the children. They will ask you bundles of questions which you have to answer. The most challenging aspect is that there is no limit to their curiosity.
Another bigger and much more of a real challenge is making the families of first generation learners understand the prowess of education and thereby its pressing need. Leaving aside all the hurdles we face, when I look back, I realise that we have enabled many visible and positive changes.
Since the day I joined, I just had a little dream that all children should be able to receive education without worrying about the shackles of child labour. DRISTI has educated many such children and spread awareness about this unfortunate societal evil.
As the name suggests, DRISTI has given them new vision-this is what I believe and they believe it too.
