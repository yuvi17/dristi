SHUBHANGI MISHRA

I am Shubhangi Mishra, a student of B.Pharm in B.I.T, MESRA.
I still remember the day I went for DRISTI induction and came to know about this organisation. The induction made me feel the need and value of community service. That was the moment I decided to join DRISTI.
Six months have passed since that day and during this time, I have seen many changes in the village and also in me. I believe that the social distance between the rural and urban community can be gradually diminished with efforts similar to ours and DRISTI is a quantum leap in this direction.
In DRISTI, we organise many events in the village to create awareness regarding various issues such as health and hygiene, human rights, child labour, women safety etc. We help children with daily studies by taking tuition classes to motivate them and also meet additional academic needs. We try to celebrate many festivals with them and also organise cultural programmes, painting and mask making competitions, science exhibitions, thereby enabling a sense of culture, fostering creativity and inquisitiveness in them.
DRISTI is now an NGO and is aimed at helping people residing in rural areas and thus contributing to the development of the country. I feel lucky to be a part of the DRISTI Family.
