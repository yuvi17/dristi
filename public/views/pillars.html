PILLARS

DISHA

DISHA means direction. So all the activities we are guiding people towards a particular approach comes under DISHA.This includes carrier counselling session and government schemes. This includes inspirational talks making people enthusiastic and dedicated for upliftment of the underprivileged society and watching it come true.

DIKSHA

DIKSHA means to initiate a new learning, this includes tuition classes, spoken English classes etc. Where we don't need four walls and a blackboard, only willing minds. DIKSHA is making the children to lean forward to catch the wings of learning, and making them awake with a shine of imagination developing with each passing day.

DASKHA

DAKSHA means skilful or competent. Events like computer education and vocational trainings completes the word DAKSHA. We empathise on creating such personality which will help in creating a positive impact on the society under the guidance of some of the brightest mind we have.

DAMINI

DAMINI or Women Empowerment wing works on the task of upliftment of women in rural areas. Empowerment of women has taken an edge in the present era. This gender is excelling in every field and proving its metal in every arena that offers competition, whether it is social, political, and administrative or others.
