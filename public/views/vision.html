OUR VISION

Everybody dreams of a utopian society but unequal division of opportunities is the harsh reality of today’s world. Even at BIT Mesra, it is a sad plight that the residents of nearby villages hardly dream to send their children to such coveted institutes. At DRISTI, we try to bridge this gap between the fortunate and the not-so fortunate people. The real India is said to reside in its villages so we begin our work from there.

WHAT WE WANT

EDUCATION
FOR ALL

Every child in the village attends school and adult education gains acceptance as it is education that takes people towards the road leading to a better life. Poverty can never be entirely eliminated without education.

SOCIAL INTEGRATION

Many villages are cut off from the mainstream society which causes them to miss out on a vast number of opportunities. Their integration into mainstream society will open new doors of prosperity for both parties.

NO GENDER DIVISION

Both genders avail equal treatment in terms of education, care and opportunities. Women can be empowered only when the gender division that starts from homes is curbed. We want to create a society with equal opportunities.

TECHNOLOGICAL EMPOWERMENT

Technology is the tool using which villages can advance in the rapidly changing world. It helps them become self-sufficient.Long term prosperity can only be achieved through self reliance that stems from technology.
